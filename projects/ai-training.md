# AI Training Platform

## Overview

A comprehensive training platform designed to democratize AI knowledge and help organizations implement AI solutions across their operations.

### Mission
Train and enable teams to build and deploy AI systems effectively in their business contexts.

---

## What's Included

### Training Materials
- **Practical Guides**: Step-by-step AI implementation tutorials
- **Case Studies**: Real-world examples from logistics, marketing, business, and education
- **Hands-on Notebooks**: Interactive Jupyter/Colab notebooks with executable code
- **Best Practices**: Production-ready patterns and architectures

### Covered Sectors
- 🚚 Logistics & Supply Chain
- 💼 Business & SMEs
- 📢 Marketing & Analytics
- 🎓 Education & Learning Systems
- 🏭 And more...

### Target Audience
- Business professionals new to AI
- SME decision-makers
- Students and educators
- Teams planning AI implementation

---

## Key Modules

### Module 1: AI Fundamentals
